6. Key Findings

FindingDetail
Collision box vs visualSedan collision (36×64) is 10-12% smaller than visual (40×70.6). Forgiving — player feels they dodged when they actually did.
Peak area at 45°Sedan AABB at 45° (73×73 = 5329) is 72% larger than at 0° (45×69 = 3105). Rotation makes the car much harder to squeeze through gaps.
Large vehicle peakTruck at 45° is 99×99 = 9801 — 2x the 0° box. Almost fills a full 2-cell × 2-cell area.
Barricade swapBarricades swap w/h at ≥45°. At 0°: 48×16 (wide). At 90°: 16×48 (tall). Simple binary — no intermediate sizes.
Shrub/Tree fixedShrub-1 (103×60) is the widest prop. Tree (88×89) is the tallest. Neither adjusts for rotation — potential mismatch if rotated.
Train leniencyTrain collision H (32px) is 27% shorter than visual (44px). Designed for fairness — trains are fast-moving.
Pillar = no collisionPillars are visual-only. Player can drive through them. Intentional or oversight?
